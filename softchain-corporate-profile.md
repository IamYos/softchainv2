## Company Overview

Softchain is an engineering firm headquartered in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, founded in 2022. We deliver software, manage IT systems, and maintain technical infrastructure for clients internationally.

We are a service company. We do not sell products. We take on projects — scoping, architecture, development, integration, deployment, and long-term support — and we deliver them with senior technical execution, clean architecture, and full ownership of the result.

Our work is organized across two disciplines: software engineering and IT systems and operational support. Software engineering covers application development across web, mobile, and backend platforms, system integration, AI-integrated development, and technical consultancy. IT covers on-premise and cloud infrastructure, networking, security, and operational support including procurement coordination.

We serve startups, founders, small and growing businesses, freelancers, and established companies. For early-stage teams without a technical co-founder or CTO, we operate at that level — owning architecture decisions, defining the product, and executing the build. For businesses that need ongoing technical support after delivery, we structure contracts around the specific requirements of each project. This includes periodic maintenance and updates, retained engineering hours for continued development, system monitoring, managed updates and releases, or full operational support depending on the engagement. The scope, terms, and model are always defined per project.

We are licensed in Dubai and deliver for clients globally. We are not limited by sector or geography.

### AI-Integrated Development

AI is part of how we build software. It is not a separate service and it is not an add-on. It is embedded in the development process where it creates real, measurable value for the product and the business operating it.

We work with the full range of AI tooling available: commercial APIs from major providers, open-source models that can be self-hosted and fully controlled, and private model deployments for clients who require strict data governance and complete infrastructure ownership.

**What we build:**

- **Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems** — language models connected to structured and unstructured knowledge bases, delivering accurate, context-aware responses grounded in actual business data rather than general model training.
- **Conversational interfaces** — AI-driven chat systems, assistants, and support agents integrated into web and mobile applications, built for production reliability and real user interaction at scale.
- **Agent-based systems** — autonomous AI agents that execute multi-step workflows, make decisions based on defined logic, interact with external tools and APIs, and operate within controlled boundaries and permissions.
- **Workflow automation** — AI-powered processing of documents, emails, forms, and unstructured data: extraction, classification, summarization, routing, and decision support built into operational pipelines.
- **Intelligent application features** — search, recommendations, content generation, predictive inputs, and context-aware functionality built directly into the product architecture rather than layered on top.

**How we deploy:**

- **Cloud-hosted inference** using commercial API providers — for use cases where speed, cost efficiency, and rapid iteration are the priority.
- **Self-hosted and on-premise deployment** — for clients with data residency requirements, regulatory constraints, or a preference to keep all data processing and model inference within their own infrastructure.
- **Hybrid architectures** — combining cloud-based and local inference depending on the sensitivity, volume, and nature of each task within the system.
- **Private model fine-tuning** — adapting base models to specific business domains, terminology, data patterns, and operational workflows for higher accuracy and relevance.

Our AI engineering work includes model selection, prompt architecture, inference pipeline design, evaluation, and continuous refinement. We select the right model for each task based on capability, cost, latency, and deployment constraints — not based on market visibility or vendor positioning.

We also apply AI within our own engineering process. Our development workflow operates as a hybrid: experienced engineers working alongside AI-assisted tooling for code generation, review, testing, and iteration. This is not a replacement for engineering judgment. It is a disciplined approach that maintains high output quality across projects while keeping architecture clean and codebases maintainable.

### Technical Consultancy

Before any build begins, we work with clients to define the technical foundation of the project.

- **Architecture design** — system structure, data models, service boundaries, and communication patterns suited to the project's actual scale and operational requirements
- **Stack selection** — languages, frameworks, databases, hosting, and tooling chosen based on real technical needs, not industry trends or vendor defaults
- **Technical scoping** — requirements broken into defined deliverables with realistic timelines and clear dependencies
- **Feasibility assessment** — evaluating whether a proposed technical approach will hold up at the required scale, budget, and timeline before committing to it
- **CTO-level partnership** — for startups and founders who need a senior technical partner to own architecture, make build-or-buy decisions, guide the product roadmap, and carry execution from concept through delivery and beyond

Consultancy at Softchain is the front end of delivery. Every recommendation we make, we are prepared to build.
